Cost control & forecasting
Real cost control, not just budget tracking. Project IQ separates what you've spent from what it's going to cost — so you see overruns before they happen.
✓
Cost codes with original budget, approved variations and current budget
✓
Forecast to Complete (FTC) — what it will cost to finish, not what's left in budget
✓
Forecast Final Cost = Spent + FTC. Variance = Budget minus FFC
✓
Commitments register — locked and expected costs tracked separately
✓
Invoice register with approval workflow and payment tracking
✓
Cashflow with 4 forecast curve types — straight line, front-loaded, back-loaded, S-curve

SOP Act compliance, built in
Every payment claim starts a 10-business-day countdown. Miss it and the full amount is deemed accepted under the Building and Construction Industry Security of Payment Act 1999 (NSW).
✓
Countdown timer starts automatically when a claim is served
✓
Alerts when deadlines are 3 days out — and again when overdue
✓
Builders respond with a full Payment Schedule — line item by line item
✓
Accept, partially approve, or reject each line with stated reasons
✓
Supports Excel breakdown uploads, inspection reports and photos
✓
Subcontractors see the same clock from their portal

Retention — stop losing track of your money
Retention is one of the most commonly lost revenue streams in construction. Project IQ tracks every dollar withheld, the cap, and the release date automatically.
✓
Auto-calculates retention withheld per claim
✓
Tracks progress to the retention cap (e.g. 5% of contract value)
✓
Configurable per contract — 10%, 5%, 2.5% or custom rate
✓
Supports cash retention, bank guarantees, and nil retention
✓
Flags the 12-month release date after Practical Completion
✓
Subcontractors see their own retention from their portal
